Summer Mobile Games Development Camp

Introduction

We have designed a 4-days mobile games development experience for you kids in this Summer! In the camp, students will learn to build an Android game from scratch using the MIT App Inventor tool. Our experienced instructors will guide the students from the very basics to building a gyro-controlled game.

Students will also be encouraged to put in their ideas and creativity in the app and share the app with friends and families.

Program Curriculum

  • Programming basics
  • Control flow (e.g. if, then, else etc.)
  • Variables and computations
  • Phone hardware sensors control (touch screen gestures, gyros, accelerometer, GPS etc.)
  • Debugging techniques
