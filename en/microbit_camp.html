Easter Micro:bit Maker Camp

Introduction

We have designed a 4-days maker experience for your kids in this Easter! In the camp, students will learn to design and build a smart home or smart city invention from scratch. Students will learn about how to control and program the sensors and motors, creating an interactive smart device. Our experienced instructors will guide your kid form the very basics to building a smart device.

Students will also be encouraged to put in their ideas and creativity in their unique invention and share it with friends and families.

Program Curriculum

  • Design Thinking
  • Programming basics
  • Control flow (e.g. if, then, else etc.)
  • Variables and computations
  • Input & output control (accelerometer, compass sensors, Bluetooth, LED display, programmable buttons)
  • Debugging techniques

Age
