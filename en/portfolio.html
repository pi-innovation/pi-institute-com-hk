CWD Primary Robotics Workshops cum Competitions

Central and Western District Office, Home Affairs Department

Our team has worked with Home Affairs Department, District Council, and The Council of CWD School Heads on "CWD Primary Robotics Workshops cum Competitions" for 4 consecutive years. The program covered 12 primary schools in the district and provided robot soccer, resuce and sumo training workshops for the students. After the training program, they will then participate in a day-long competition, competing with their fellow teams from other schools.

Dah Sing Life Assurance LEGO Workshops

Dah Sing Life Assurance

Pi Innovation co-organized LEGO workshops with Dah Sing Life Assurance in life insurance briefing sessions, allowing parents in learning about insurance policies for their family without boring their kids.

The participating kids need to create their own LEGO "Downhill Racer" and compete on a downhill track to see whose car could run fastest without damage! They also got chances in designing program for robotics arms.

Humanoid Robots Workshop

Jordan Valley St. Joseph's Catholic Primary School

Pi Innovation partnered with Jordan Valley St. Joseph's Catholic Primary School on humanoid robots workshop for primary school students. The students were able to experience how complicated it is to teach a robot in doing simple tasks like standing, walking or waving hands. Within a day, they will need to assemble the humanoid robots as well as progamming it so that it would be able to walk steadily and avoid obstacles.
