Education Internship Program

Program Objective

Provide students who are studying science and engineering an opportunity to gain practical teaching experiences and knowledge and help them in landing an teaching career.

Target Students

Current student from science or engineering related majors. Interested in joining education field or becoming teacher after graduation

Program Details

Interns will be renewing their contract every half year. Interns will receive training on teaching, classroom control, courseware development and other education-related topics. Apart from the training, interns will be working with our experienced instructors on designing STEM courseware and events as well as teaching in NGOs, education institutions and schools.

Interns will also get chances to participate in STEM education events and symposiums organized by local tertiary education institutions.

Working Hours

Around 5 hours per week. Flexible.

Program Schedule

Start from Sept/Oct 2018

Positions

Interns will be assigned roles according to their experiences, skills & knowledge and year of study. Roles included Instructor, Senior Instructor, Courseware Developer and Course Coordinator (ranked by ascending seniority). Roles will be reviewed on every half year upon contract review.

Application Deadline

28th Sept 2018
